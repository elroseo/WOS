## Goal

Understand how enterprise customers structure their GitHub presence, critical for CRE work.

## Key concepts

- Organization vs enterprise account hierarchy
- Organization roles: owner, member, billing manager, security manager
- Enterprise roles and enterprise-level policies
- Teams and team permissions
- SAML SSO and SCIM at the org and enterprise level
- Managed user accounts (EMU)

## Learning tasks

- [ ] Understand what enterprise policies override org-level settings
- [ ] Learn the difference between regular accounts and Enterprise Managed Users (EMU)
