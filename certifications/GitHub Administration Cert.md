## Why this matters for CREs

Administration is at the heart of CRE work. Enterprise customers rely on you for guidance on org structure, policies, authentication, and GHES management. This cert validates the skills you'll use daily.

## Study checklist

- [ ] Organization and enterprise account management
- [ ] User roles and permissions (org, repo, enterprise)
- [ ] Authentication: SAML SSO, SCIM, EMU
- [ ] Repository administration and rulesets
- [ ] GitHub Enterprise (Cloud and Server) management
- [ ] Audit logging and compliance
- [ ] Billing and licensing

## Pillar connections

| Topic | CRE Pillar | How it helps |
